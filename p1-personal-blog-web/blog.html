Hue Imperial City is a Hue tourist destination that converges the pinnacle architectural beauty of the Nguyen Dynasty feudal era, built hundreds of years ago with an extremely large area, with more than 100 splendid architectural works such as: Ngo Mon Gate, Dien Tho Palace, Forbidden City, Thai Hoa Palace arranged harmoniously and reasonably.
